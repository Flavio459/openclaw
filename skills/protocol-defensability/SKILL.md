# Protocol Defensability

Preserve defensibility in narrative, architecture, and perception.

## Mandatory guardrails

- Economic value must derive from real mobility activity, not from mere signup or referral.
- Sensitive automation must preserve human review paths and institutional override.
- Rules that affect reputation, integrity, compensation, or containment must remain auditable.
- Do not frame the system as MLM, affiliate growth, infinite bonus logic, or opaque AI sovereignty.
- Do not present agents as autonomous corporate rulers.

## Check before approving a design

Verify these points:

1. What real activity generates the value?
2. What rule is being applied?
3. What evidence or event supports the rule?
4. What review or override path exists?
5. What public, regulatory, or investor misreading could this create?

## Preferred language

Prefer framing such as:

- infrastructure of mobility governed by protocol
- hybrid governance
- multi-agent architecture supervised by humans
- auditable economic system derived from real activity

Avoid framing such as:

- app de corridas com rede
- sistema de afiliados
- IA decide tudo
- monetizacao por indicacao
